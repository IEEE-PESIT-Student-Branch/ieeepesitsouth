Kalpana is a one day Technical Exhibition cum Humanitarian event of the Student Branch WiE chapter, with the sole motive of empowering women in the fields of Science and Engineering. Following last year's mammoth success with a massive number of ideas and brilliant showcases, we return this year, with the same core principles but enhanced enthusiasm.

Kalpana | Exhibition

A Project Exhibition that provides a platform to students to solve real world problems that currently plague the society. Engineering undergraduate students across Karnataka can register and showcase their projects which solve real world problems currently existing in the society.

Cash Prize:

  1. Best Project Overall : ₹20,000/-*
  2. Runner up : ₹10,000/-*

    3. As an event under WiE, we value and motivate women to enter into the field of Science and Technology, which is why an Encouragement Award* for the 'Best All Girls Team’ has been instituted.

Competition Entry Fee:-

  1. ₹160 for IEEE Student Members
  2. ₹200 for other Students

Competition Details:-

  1. Date : 6th of October, 2018
  2. Venue : PES University - Electronic City Campus, Hosur Road, Bangalore
  3. For details and Registrations, log on to : kalpana.ieeepesitsouth.org

Kalpana | Humanitarian

More than 80 students from an underdeveloped government high school (9th and 10th grade) in Bangalore will be invited and delivered talks by experienced speakers regarding their career and future. This is done in order to encourage and motivate them to complete their formal education after high school, which is pivotal to the advancement of the society. The registration money we get will be used to provide food and stationery items for the government school students.
