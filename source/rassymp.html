Robotics and Automation Symposium

Date : 28th April, 2018

The IEEE PESIT South Campus Student Branch and the IEEE Robotics and Automation Society Bangalore Chapter, are jointly organising a symposium that will pivot around the central theme of robotics and automation. Boasting of noteworthy speakers, there's much to take away from this educational conference.

Some of the major areas covered are :-

  1. Computer vision in Robotics
  2. Robotics simulation
  3. Artificial Intelligence
  4. Autonomous Vehicles
  5. Space Robotics
