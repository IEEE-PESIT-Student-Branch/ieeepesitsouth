Photonics and Nanotechnology Symposium

Date : 7th October, 2017

Objectives:

  1. Understanding the concept/definition of Photonics
  2. Relationships of photoics (Classical optics, modern optics, quantum optics, etc.)
  3. Application (Military, avionics, commercial, etc.)
  4. Research in Photonics
  5. The electron – photon analogy.
  6. Understanding the concept/definition of Nanotechnology
  7. The fundamental principles of nano materials and nano-electronics
  8. Applications
  9. Research in nanotech
  10. Relationship with photonics, interaction of EMR and materials using FEKO simulation tool.

Speakers:

  1. Dr. Kaustav Bhowmick
  2. Dr. Preetha Sharan
  3. Dr. Gajanan V Honnavar
  4. Dr. Revanasidappa M

Participation and charges:

  1. A total of 100 delegates to be accommodated.
  2. Professional delegates entry ticket at ₹450.
  3. IEEE Student member entry at ₹200
  4. Student entry ticket for non-IEEE members and IEEE Professionals at ₹300.

Timeline:

  1. Registrations : Online, via form on the website : within 28th Sept - 5th Oct
  2. Fee payment : Online* : 28th Sept - 5th Oct
  3. Invoice and Itenary : 7th Oct
  4. Event : 7th October, 2017
