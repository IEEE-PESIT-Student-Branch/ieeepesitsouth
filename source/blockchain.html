Blockchain Workshop



Date : 17th Feburary, 2018

IEEE PESIT South Campus brings to you, a one-day workshop on Blockchain technology at PES Institute of Technology - South Campus, Bangalore. Blockchain refers to a continuously growing list of records, called blocks, which are linked and secured using cryptography. By allowing digital information to be distributed but not copied, blockchain technology created the backbone of a new type of internet.

Speakers and Resource Person

  1. Mr. Sajeev Vishwanathan, CEO, New Street Technologies, Bangalore
  2. Mr. Promod Gururaj, CTO, New Street Technologies, Bangalore

Objectives

  1. Key components of Blockchain
  2. Blockchain versus Crypocurrencies
  3. Types of Blockchain
  4. What can and what cannot Blockchain do in the real world
  5. Issues to be aware of

Hands On session

  1. Usecase to develop
  2. The actual mechanism of running nodes on a private chain
  3. Initiating a transaction and seeing the creation of Blocks.

Entry Passes and Fee:

  1. ₹200 for IEEE Student Members
  2. ₹350 for other Students of PESIT South Campus
